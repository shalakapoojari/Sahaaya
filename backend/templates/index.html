Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to use Sahayaa?
No! You can use any Sahayaa pod as a guest — no registration, no login, no personal data stored. Accounts are optional and let you track history and earn community badges.
What does "Free Access Zone" mean?
Some pods are sponsored by community members or organizations. In these zones, all products are available at zero cost. Look for the 💝 badge on the machines page.
How does the Quick Exit button work?
Pressing 🚪 QUICK EXIT (or the Esc key) instantly hides the screen with a privacy overlay — useful if you need discretion in public spaces. Click anywhere to restore.
Is my transaction data private?
Guest transactions are stored anonymously with no PII. If you're logged in, only you can see your history from your profile.
